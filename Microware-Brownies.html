Microware Brownies



Microware Brownies

    Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup of self-raising flour
  • 1/4 cup of caster suger
  • 2 tablespoon of cocoa
  • 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoon of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of icing suger

    • Equipment tools:

  • cup
  • Measuring cup
  • Teaspoon
  • Tablespoon
  • Microware

    Let Create The Recipe:

  1. Add 1/4 cup of self-raising flour.
  2. Add 1/4 cup of caster suger.
  3. Add 2 tablespoon of cocoa.
  4. Add 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  5. Add 3 tablespoon of water.
  6. Add 1/2 teaspoon of icing suger.
  7. Now mix all the ingredients together until it have a smooth mixture.
  8. And place into the microwave on high for 1 minute and 40 seaconds.
  9. And dusting it with a little icing suger.
