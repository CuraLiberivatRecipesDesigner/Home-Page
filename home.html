    The Making of the Recipe: 

     

Info: I also remake this 18th-century recipe of the American apple pie into a modernised 

version of itself with a new design. I enhanced the original 18th century American Apple

Pie recipe by adding more modern steps and measurements that were not in the original 18th

century American Apple Pie recipe. And the apple pie recipe comes from the book called The

First American Cookbook by Amelia Simmons. I combine both the recipes called The First 

American Cook Book by Amelia Simmons was made in 1796. And then I add in an unknown 1783 

unknown pie paste recipe that I don't know where it came from? And the custard is a 1769 

recipe from a book called The English Housekeeper by Elizabeth Raffald.
